Baked Salmon with
Lemon & Dill

Medium 25 minutes

A healthy, protein-rich dinner with tender salmon baked in zesty lemon and fresh dill.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. Place salmon on a baking sheet lined with parchment.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Top with lemon slices and dill.
  5. Bake for 15–18 minutes or until cooked through.
